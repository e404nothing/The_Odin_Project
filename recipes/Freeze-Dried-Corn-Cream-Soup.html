Freeze Dried Corn Cream Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of water
  • 1.25 cup freeze dried half and half cream
  • 1/4 cup Chicken Soup Mix (https://www.bulkbarn.ca/en/Products/All/Chicken-Soup-Mix-426)
  • 1.25 cup crushed dried corn
  • .25 cup of freeze dried potatoes (4:1 ratio)
  • 1/2 cup of dried corn
  • 1 Cup of dried carrots
  • 1/2 cup of bacon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the water, half and half cream, Chicken Soup Mix, crushed dried corn, freeze dried potatoes, dried corn, dried carrots, bacon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the soup is hot and the flavors have melded together.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
