Thai Green Curry

Ingredients:

  • Green chilis
  • Curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables of your choice
  • Rice
  • Fresh lime

Directions:

  1. Prepare your vegetables and chicken. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces and the chicken into strips or cubes.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and add the curry paste. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until it's no longer pink.
  4. Add the vegetables to the pan and stir to combine with the chicken and curry paste.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cook until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Serve the curry over rice with a squeeze of fresh lime.
